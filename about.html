Crafting artistic meaningful games



Hi there, I'm Yidi! I am a passionate game designer and programmer. I'm currently pursuing my Master of Entertainment Technology degree at the Entertainment Technology Center of Carnegie Mellon University. I dedicate myself to making fun and meaningful games.

As a game designer, I see games as art and an extremely expressive medium. I love making visually stunning games with innovative interactions and rich feedback that invoke something deep from players' mind, and I enjoy tricky design challenge through collaboration, playtesting and iterations.

As a game programmer, I am fascinated with the power of programming and am always willing to pick up new skills and experiment with new technologies. Thanks to my background in building engineering, I am highly skilled in calculus, engineering mathematics, and classical mechanics, which makes it easier for me to pick up new programming skills.
