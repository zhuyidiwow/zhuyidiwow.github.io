General Information

Project Duration 5 months
Team Size 6
Genre Puzzle
Platform Andoird/iOS

Ripple Blossom was first prototyped during Global Game Jam 2017 Hong Kong site, under the theme of "Waves". This was my first game jam and first time working in ! However, our team got the Best Theme Integration award!

The theme of this year is "waves". We made a game about ripples in a pool. Players use the power of ripples to wave sleeping lotus. Here is the video.
