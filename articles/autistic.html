Using Technology to Facilitate Communication and Relationships

Project Info

Duration 15 weeks (Jan 15 - May 5, 2018)
Team Size 5
Team members Ridima, Xueyang Wang, Dan Wolpow, Yutian Zheng

Introduction

This is a semester long project at the ETC. Our team work with Beech Bottom Primary School, our project advisors, and the advisory board to design and implement an interactive experience for grade 1-4 students to understand students on the autism spectrum see, hear, and feel life.

More details will be available as we progress.
