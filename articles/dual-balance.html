Brief Description

This is a 2-player cooperative mobile game for COMP3432 Innovative Computing Paradigm class at HK PolyU.

We decided to make this game because we miss the old days when there were plenty of local multi-player games. Nowadays we are getting better and better graphics, but only few of them focus on local co-operative play. So we made a casual game in which players can beat levels together with their friends in a happy and relaxing atmosphere.

We focus on making mechanics and levels that require co-operations. To support the objective, we took a simple approach and split the control to two players:

Player 1 controls the left and right movement, and player 2 controls the up and down movement. They can raise their phones quickly together to make the ball jump.

We got some playtesters played this game, most agree that this game is super fun and the experience of playing it strengthened their relationship with friends.
