Brief Description

SHU is a Two-player Oculus Game where players cooperate with each other to overcome obstacles and reach their destination. Players will build up relationship during the journey and experience the world with stunning visuals, haunting music and unique gameplay.

Design Phiolosophy

I firmly believe that each game should have a clear design goal that every design decision should serve, and several design pillars that every design decision should follow.

Design Problems and Solutions

Get the player look around

Damage players in VR

Problem 1: Unlike games on PC, it's tricky to like the players know that they are damaged in VR. On PC, designers can use full screen effects like in Call of Duty series, or screen shaking, or move players around depending on the damage source. In VR, if any one of these are designed and implemented improperly, motion sickness will come to the player quickly.

Solution 1: The solution we came up with is to use post-processing effects + sound effects + slight movement of player.
